Trails

 

In addition to the maps included as pdf files on this site, we are pleased to present new trail maps using current GPS and mapping programs.  Groton resident, Chris Marsh, has done a yeoman’s job assembling the trail maps accessible.  Chris has taken on this endeavor to assist him in completing his Eagle Scout project and we are all grateful for his work. 

Please be advised that the material posted on this website is for informational purposes only and that the Town of Groton and the Groton Trails Committee disclaim liability in the connection with the use of land.

Looking to help

The Groton Trails Committee is always looking for help mapping trails.  Do you have a GPS unit (handheld, watch, etc.) and would you like to help?  Walk or run one of the many trails in Groton using your unit.  Mark waypoints (points of interest), take pictures, write down comments, etc.  Export the track and waypoint information from your GPS unit preferably in .gpx format, but .csv, .kmz, .tpo, and other formats can be used.

Send us your information, files, pictures, notes, etc. along with any other information you would like to provide.  If we have questions, we’ll contact you for more information.
