Mountain Biking Trails

Groton Town Forest

Smooth flowy trails that are suitable for all abilities. Whether it's the network of cart roads or the 8 miles of single track, all routes lead to big smiles. For those looking for a longer route head north to Fitch's Bridge, cross over and head south to return. Parking is at the end of Town Forest Road.

New England Mountain Biking Association

Wharton Plantation

Some of the more challenging trails in town, Wharton's 5-6 miles of rocky terrain will test even the most accomplished riders. Extend the ride by venturing over to Crystal Spring. Park at the junction of Martin Pond Rd and Rocky Hill rd.

Map

Williams Barn

A well marked network of trails for all abilities. Venture across Chicopee Row to Fitch Woods and McLain's Woods makes for 5-6 miles of mostly single track trail. Chestnut Hill is a challenging climb that rewards with a nice view to the east. Parking at Williams Barn off Chicopee Row.
