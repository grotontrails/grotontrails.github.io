A hearty group of 8 trail enthusiasts met at Williams Barn to commence work on cleaning up the trails from last week’s ice storm.  The foot of snow from last evening made work difficult but offered a tranquil setting. 


It will take months to clean up all the trails so if you go for a hike please pick up debris and toss it to the side of the trail.
