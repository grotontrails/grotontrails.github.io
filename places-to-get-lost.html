Places To Get Lost In

Town Forest

The Groton Town Forest is one of the largest trail networks in Groton. This network of trails is truly very diverse and will suit anyoneâ€™s wishes. The twisting trails and intertwined loops will provide a challenge to even the most experienced hiker. Whether you want to go for a stroll on a welcoming summer day or tear it up on a mountain bike this property is for you. There is a scenic route that follows the Nashua River as well as an alluring trail that follows the old railroad tracks. A walk in the Groton Town Forest is truly wonderful as you let your worries and obligations fade away and just enjoy the untouched beauty of nature.

Not only do we recommend that you get lost here on foot and bike, but feel free to bring a buddy to get lost with. Whether that buddy be a person, dog, horse, or all of the above, we do not judge.
